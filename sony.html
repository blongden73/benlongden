Case Study

— Sony Music

The insight and development platform was designed to enable the users at Sony music to engage with the huge amount of content that the Insight and development team were producing on a regular basis. The solution was to design a platform which went beyond an intranet, and certainly beyond a file system. It was designed to be a dashboard that felt alive, with data driven by the uploading of content and featured editorial case studies which aimed to be of interest to anyone who came to the platform and drive onward journeys to related content.

A platform for data, insights & music

  • Art Direction

Smart search led

The platform was designed to be search first. The main goal for the platform was for people to be able to find the resources they would normally email to get hold of, often multiple times. The seacrh was designed to be prominent, and prompt people for the types of things they could search for. It also served the purpose of introducing people to the purpose of the platform.

Templated blocks rather than pages

The system for the design was based around repeatable square blocks. These blocks could be combined in different orders to build pages which ultimatley felt more suitable for the content rather than trying to crowbar the content into a rigid page template.

This principle was rolled out beyond the platform and into the presentation and document templates which would be used for the content of the site.

EQ the brand

Designed to be in a similar graphic world to the 4th floor creative branding but with iconography that sets the Audience Development Team in its own space. The naming of the platform led direclty to a response which felt like it needed movement. The brand pulls from key icon visuals related to music, and it's structure lends itself to the digital design system which followed.
